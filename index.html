Affordable & Clean Energy

Hello! Energy powers our lights, heats our homes, and helps us learn and play. The Sun, Wind, and Water give us clean energy that doesn’t hurt the Earth!

What is clean energy?

  • 🌞 Solar: Sunlight powers solar panels.
  • 🌬️ Wind: Wind spins big fans called turbines to make electricity.
  • 💧 Water: Rivers can turn machines to create power.

Clean energy is safe, powerful, and helps keep our planet happy and healthy!
